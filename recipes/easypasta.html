Easy pasta recipe!

Garlick and oil pasta!

This is a very easy and practical recipe for those days when we need to hurry

As easy as it is, it can still be a very good dish and there are some hints that can really spice it up

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • Oil of your choice
  • Grated parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil

Steps!

  1. Start by boiling enough water to cook your pasta
  2. Chop the garlic into fine pieces
  3. Add the oil to a pan and let it get hot for 15 to 20 seconds
  4. Add the garlic to the pan
  5. When you see that the garlic is starting to change color it's time to turn off the heat
  6. When your pasta is done cooking, pour 3 to 4 spoons of the cooking water into the pan. CAREFULLY!
  7. Add the pasta into the pan and stir it
  8. If you want you can add the grated parmesan cheese now and stir it a little more so it melts
  9. Finally put it on a plate and place some fresh basil on top. You can also add more parmesan cheese
  10. Enjoy!
