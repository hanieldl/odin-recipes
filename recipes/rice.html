Rice!

Very easy rice recipe

This one is for those fresh college students that really know nothing about cooking!

A well made rice can be a real life saver. Add a steak or some boiled vegetables and you are almost done!

Ingredients

  • Rice of your choice. It can be white rice, brown rice, black rice, etc.
  • Salt
  • Garlic (optional)

Steps!

  1. Start by boiling enough water to cook the rice. It's usually a 2 for 1 measure. So if you want to cook one cup of rice you need 2 cups of water
  2. If you want to, you can add some cloves for garlic into the boiling water along with the salt (you usually want 1 teaspoon of salt for every cup of rice
  3. Add the rice and leave it to cook
  4. If you see that the water is not enough to soften the rice you can add some more. Different kinds of rice may need a little bit more water
  5. When the water is almost gone, turn off the head and close the lid. Let the rice rest for 15 to 20 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
